FAQ
07Questions

Common questions

How long does a typical project take?
A standard business website takes 2–4 weeks. Custom web or mobile applications typically take 6–12 weeks depending on scope. We commit to a timeline in writing before we start.
What does the process look like after I contact you?
We schedule a free discovery call within 24 hours, send you a detailed proposal with scope and pricing within 3 days, and can usually begin work within a week of sign-off.
Do I own the code and design?
Yes — 100%. On final payment, all source code, design files, and accounts are transferred to you. No lock-in, ever.
Can you take over or fix an existing project?
Absolutely. We start with a free code and infrastructure audit, then give you an honest recommendation: improve what exists or rebuild where it saves you money long-term.
What happens after launch?
Every plan includes free support (1–3 months). After that, our maintenance plans cover updates, backups, monitoring, and small improvements — or we hand over clean documentation if you'd rather manage it in-house.
How do payments work?
Typically 40% to begin, 30% at design approval, and 30% at launch. Enterprise engagements can be structured monthly. We accept bank transfer and UPI.
